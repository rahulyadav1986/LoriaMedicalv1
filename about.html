Dr. Victor Loria, D.O., leads the practice of Loria Medical with locations throughout the U.S. and soon, internationally. In 2010, Dr. Loria began to focus his practice in male penile shaft, penile glans, and scrotal enhancements using his Permanent Dermal Filler Injection Technique.

Dr. Loria and his team of highly trained cosmetic surgeons perform thousands of male enhancement procedures annually. Patients from all over the world seek out Loria Medical for Dr. Loria’s minimally invasive, Permanent Dermal Filler Injection Technique. As an experienced osteopathic physician and a highly regarded cosmetic surgeon, Dr. Loria has been a pioneer in the field of penis and scrotal enlargement.

Dr. Loria continues to innovate procedures in his field, designing and patenting medical devices for men to safely enlarge and lengthen their penis.

In 2014, he introduced a new medically designed Penis Pump, which was awarded a U.S. patent. In 2016 and 2017, Dr. Loria continued his research and developed the Penis Stretcher and Flaccid Penis Insert medical devices, which were also awarded U.S. patents. Dr. Loria, through Loria Medical, will soon introduce a new penis shaft lengthening device. This device will bring real results to those who are seeking a longer penis both flaccid and erect. Dr. Loria has additional patents both pending and approved for hair implants and hair extensions. These additional cosmetic enhancement products are a part of Dr. Loria’s and Loria Medical’s commitment to advancing the patients options to both look and feel better about themselves.

Dr. Loria continues to innovate and optimize non-surgical techniques that achieve natural-looking erectile enlargement, including his proven Permanent Dermal Filler Injection Technique. Loria Medical also includes procedures to help patients with erectile dysfunction and other male related complaints through customized medical plans.
